## Why we built this

We were looking for tools to help ourselves trade better. But we didn't want to leave HyperLiquid. That's where we were doing most of our trading, and everything we found wanted to take you somewhere we weren't comfortable trading in.

So we set out to build a tool that makes HyperLiquid better, not one that replaces it.

## What we believe

Perps are eating the world of finance. Traders who don't develop a real understanding of how to trade them will not be able to participate in the future of finance. AI will greatly augment that edge for individual traders, and most of them don't have access to it yet.

## Where we're going

The long term vision is a tool that trades completely autonomously on your behalf. Before Maximus can do that, it needs to understand how you trade. We help you build your unique edge and trading style first. Then we help you automate it.
