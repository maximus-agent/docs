## The team

Maximus is built by Rusty, Andy, and a team of crypto developers, community builders, and advisors.

Rusty and Andy are DeFi veterans. Since 2019 they've been building staking infrastructure, spot decentralized exchanges, and derivatives protocols across EVM and SVM chains.

The broader team brings experience from some of the largest exchanges, market makers, and consumer brands in crypto.
