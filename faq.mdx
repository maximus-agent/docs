---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Common questions about Maximus."
---

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Does Maximus replace HyperLiquid?">
    No. Maximus is a Chrome Extension that runs as an overlay on top of HyperLiquid. Your HyperLiquid interface stays exactly as it is. Maximus adds to it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which browsers are supported?">
    Any Chromium browser. Chrome and Brave are both supported. There is no Firefox or Safari support at this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to connect a wallet?">
    Yes. During setup, Maximus will ask you to sign a message with your wallet to approve a HyperLiquid agent wallet for trading. This is a one-time step. See the [Installation guide](/getting-started/installation) for the full flow.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Maximus work on mobile?">
    No. Maximus is a browser extension and desktop only. There is no mobile support at this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I turn off auto-charting?">
    Yes. Every chart tool is an individual toggle. Type `/` in the prompt bar to open the skills menu and turn any tool on or off. You can also run the Clear All skill to remove everything from your chart at once.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does voice work in all languages?">
    Maximus supports voice input in 25+ languages and automatically detects the language you speak. Text input works in most world languages. Speak or type in your language and Maximus responds in kind.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the trade journal capture all my trades automatically?">
    Yes, every trade on HyperLiquid is logged automatically. If you place trades through Maximus, it also captures your trade intent, which improves the quality of your journal analysis and reviews over time. Trades placed outside of Maximus are still logged, but without that intent context.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Maximus have access to my funds?">
    No. Maximus uses a HyperLiquid agent wallet, which is a permissioned signer that can only execute trades on your behalf. It cannot withdraw funds, does not hold any of your assets, and never has access to your private keys. You can revoke access at any time directly from HyperLiquid.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get support?">
    Reach out on [X](https://x.com/MaximusTradeX) or email us at [support@maximus.trade](mailto:support@maximus.trade). Both channels are monitored for bugs, questions, and feature requests.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
