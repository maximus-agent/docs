## What it does

Maximus logs every trade you make on HyperLiquid automatically. No manual input required. Over time it builds a picture of how you trade and surfaces patterns in your behavior, both good and bad.

## What gets logged

For every trade, Maximus records:

- Entry and exit size
- P&L
- Time of the trade
- Your thesis and whether it played out

## What Maximus surfaces

As your journal builds up, Maximus starts to show you things about your trading you might not have noticed:

- Patterns in your behavior over time
- Your winning theses and what they have in common
- The assets and setups you trade best
- Your best trades and what made them work
- Where and when you tend to lose
- Specific areas to improve

## How to access it

Just ask. A few ways to get started:

> How am I trading recently?
> Can you share my recent trading journal?
> What patterns do you see in my trades?
> When do I tend to lose?

## Data export

There is no data export at this time.
