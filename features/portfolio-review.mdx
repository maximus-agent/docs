## What it does

Ask Maximus for a portfolio review and it runs through all your open positions. For each one, it tells you how it's performing, whether the original thesis still holds, and what news or technical factors you should be watching right now.

It's the first thing to run when you sit down to trade.

## What you get

For each open position, Maximus gives you:

- Current performance and where price sits relative to your entry
- Whether the trade thesis has been invalidated by new price action or news
- Any positions that need your attention
- Relevant news, technical levels, or market conditions to watch

## How to use it

Type or say "portfolio review" in the prompt bar. Maximus pulls your current positions from HyperLiquid and runs the analysis.

You can also ask about a single position directly. "How is my BTC trade looking?" gives you the same read on just that one.
