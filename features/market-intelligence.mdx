## What it does

Ask Maximus anything about the market and it gives you a direct answer. What's driving BTC right now, why ETH is moving, what the technical picture looks like on any asset. All without opening a new tab or leaving your trading interface.

## Example questions

> What's driving the price of Bitcoin right now?
> Why is ETH dumping?
> What does the technical setup look like on SOL across multiple timeframes?
> Is there any news on RUNE today?
> What are the key levels to watch on BTC this week?

## What it draws on

Maximus has web search capability and pulls from live news sources, so the data is as fresh as possible. It can also run technical analysis across multiple timeframes to give you both a fundamental and technical read on any market movement.

## Limitations

Maximus does not have access to X, so it cannot pull crypto Twitter sentiment or surface what traders are saying right now on social media. If that's something you'd like to see, [let us know](https://x.com/MaximusTradeX).
