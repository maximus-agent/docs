## What Maximus does

Maximus is a Chrome Extension that runs directly inside HyperLiquid. It augments your trading interface with AI agents that handle charting, suggested trade setups, market research, and trade journaling. Everything you used to do manually, or skip entirely, Maximus does for you.

## How it works

Install the extension. Open HyperLiquid. Maximus appears as an overlay, docked alongside your trade panel or repositioned wherever you want it. No new tabs, no extra platforms, no switching between tools. Everything happens inside the interface you already use.

Type a command, speak one, or just ask a question. Maximus works alongside HyperLiquid, not in place of it.

## Who it's built for

Any trader on HyperLiquid who wants an edge. Whether you're looking for what to trade next or supercharging a system that already works, Maximus adds a layer of AI intelligence that most traders don't have access to.
